The meaning of the game?

You are a thief. Not just a simple one, but one of the contenders for the title of "King of thieves”. Your goal is to be no less successful than three other thieves, who in turn pursue the same goal as you - to rob your enemies and get the title of "the King of Thieves".

Purpose of the game?

Each player has 3 gems in storage and 3 empty display cases waiting for valuables. You need to go through enemy bases, collect one different stone from each and bring them back to your base. Whoever does it fastest is the real “King of Thieves”!

The rules of the game?

The rules of the game are simple. You just have to use your own inventory correctly to achieve your goals! The strongest will win!
