The meaning of the game?

You are a thief. And not simple, but one of contenders for the title of “ the King of thieves”. Three successful thieves have become your target, who persecute the same goals as you. Rob your enemies and get the title of “the King of thieves”.

The goal of the game?

Every player has 3 precious stones in the storage and 3 empty showcases, which expect values. You have to go through the enemy bases, collect one stone each of the rivals and bring them back to your base. Whoever did it faster is the real “the King of thieves”!

The rules of the game?

THe rules of the game are simple. You just have to use own inventory correctly to achieve your goals! The strongest will win!
